About Us

#SaveTheAnimals is on a mission to teach people who want to adopt. #SaveTheAnimals strives to provide trustworthy and important information about the diffuculties caused from overpopulation in kennels. We hope to bring awareness on the reasons dogs act a certain way, and the importance of spaying and neutering.
